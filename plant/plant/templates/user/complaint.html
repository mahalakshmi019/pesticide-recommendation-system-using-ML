FAQ

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI model is trained on thousands of plant images and has an accuracy rate of 92% for common plant diseases. However, we always recommend consulting with agricultural experts for critical decisions.

How do I improve detection results?

For best results, use clear photos in natural daylight, focus on affected areas, and ensure the entire leaf is visible in the image.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.

Can I use the app offline?

Currently, the app requires an internet connection. We're working on an offline version for future releases.
